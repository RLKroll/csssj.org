Give a gift membership to the CSSSJ!

To give a gift membership, fill in all of the necessary fields in the form, then click the "Submit Application" button at the bottom of the form to submit your application electronically to the CSSSJ membership director. Shortly afterward, you will receive an email from the membership director containing an address to which you must send a check made payable to "CSSSJ" in the amount shown in step 2 of the form. The application is not complete until your check is received and processed. Once your check is received, you will be provided with a certificate that you can give to the recipient.

PLEASE READ: CSSSJ Membership is good for 12 months after dues are paid.
