Succulent Gardening in Limited Space

Come join us for a demonstration of potting/repotting plants in limited space. Don’t have an outdoor space or weather too cold? There will be suggestions for planting indoors and in small areas for limited clean up.

We will also cover some tips and tricks for accomplishing these tasks more easily.

Audience participation encouraged. Bring your favorite planting tools to show.

The presenter will be Loyd Monaco, CSSSJ Past President.
